In an Emergency

Call:

911


310-6789


1-800-784-2433

(1-800-SUICIDE)

- If you suspect that your life or the life of your friend or family member is in immediate danger.

- If you are in distress and need to talk with someone through a crisis

Do not add an area code. This number will connect to a BC crisis line with no wait.

- If you are having suicidal thoughts but don't think your life is in immediate danger.

The call is free anytime of the day or night.
